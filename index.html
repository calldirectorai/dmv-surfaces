Our Story

Built on Expertise, Driven by Quality

DMV Surfaces was founded by Arash Moazzez with a clear vision: deliver commercial flooring projects that exceed expectations in quality, timeline, and budget. Based in Ashburn, Virginia, we serve clients across DC, Maryland, and Virginia.

With over 30 years of combined experience in the commercial flooring industry, our team has installed, restored, and consulted on hundreds of projects—from corporate headquarters to hospitality venues, healthcare facilities, and commercial properties of all types.

Our recent expansion into strategic consulting services allows us to bring our deep product knowledge and field expertise to architects, designers, and property managers who need independent guidance on flooring specifications, warranty navigation, and project planning.

Services

Consulting Services

Product Specification

Independent, unbiased recommendations for the optimal flooring systems tailored to your environment, traffic levels, performance needs, durability requirements, and budget.

Moisture & Subfloor Evaluation

Thorough moisture testing and subfloor assessment with clear remediation recommendations to prevent costly installation failures before they occur.

Inspection Services

Comprehensive pre-installation assessments, progress monitoring, quality control checks, and final acceptance evaluations.

Budget Planning

Accurate project budgeting with lifecycle cost analysis to ensure realistic planning and maximum long-term value.

Maintenance Planning

Customized long-term maintenance programs, including daily/weekly protocols, deep cleaning schedules, and preventive care strategies to extend flooring lifespan and protect your investment.

Failure Analysis

Expert investigation of flooring failures, including moisture issues, adhesive breakdown, premature wear, installation defects, and substrate problems.

Warranty Navigation

Professional guidance on warranty claims, required documentation, manufacturer disputes, and effective resolution strategies.

Expert Testimony

Clear, professional opinions, detailed reports, and testimony support for disputes, insurance claims, warranty conflicts, and legal proceedings.
