About DMV Surfaces

Full-service commercial flooring company headquartered in Ashburn, VA. Over 30 years of combined expertise.

Our Story

Built on Expertise, Driven by Quality

DMV Surfaces was founded by Arash Moazzez with a clear vision: deliver commercial flooring projects that exceed expectations in quality, timeline, and budget. Based in Ashburn, Virginia, we serve clients across DC, Maryland, and Virginia.

With over 30 years of combined experience in the commercial flooring industry, our team has installed, restored, and consulted on hundreds of projects—from corporate headquarters to hospitality venues, healthcare facilities, and commercial properties of all types.

Our recent expansion into strategic consulting services allows us to bring our deep product knowledge and field expertise to architects, designers, and property managers who need independent guidance on flooring specifications, warranty navigation, and project planning.

New Service

Expert Guidance Without Manufacturer Bias

Our consulting division leverages 30+ years of installation and product knowledge to help you make better flooring decisions. We work independently—no manufacturer affiliations that could compromise our recommendations.

Whether you need help specifying the right product for a challenging environment, navigating a warranty claim, or understanding why a floor failed—we provide clear, actionable guidance.

Consulting Services

Product Specification

Independent product recommendations based on your specific environment, traffic patterns, and performance requirements.
